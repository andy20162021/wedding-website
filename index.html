Things to do

If you are visiting Santa Cruz

  • Capitola is a great town to spend time and take photos (but parking is really tough there).
  • You can take the Roaring Camp Trip and let us know how it goes (we booked the ticket last time, but the trip was cancelled due to storm).
  • It is always relaxing to just walk around the beach.
  • You can take a great dinner at Shadowsbrook restaurant.
  • If you want Chinese food, 四姐 Special Noodle is a good choice.

If you also want to visit Bay Area

  • Lake Tahoe is a great place to visit in the summer. You cannot miss the clear Kayak there.
  • Filoli is one of the best places we have found to take photos.
  • Napa Valley is a beautiful place to visit even if you are not a drinker.
  • City Lights Bookstore is Bo's hidden-gem in SF.
  • You can spend a whole afternoon in Berekeley by chating with local store owners and learn about their life story.
  • 山城辣妹子 is our favorite hotpot place and Boiling Beijing is always our goto for authentic Chinese food.
