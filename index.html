We first crossed paths in Berkeley and officially became a couple on May 19, 2021. Since then, every day has been filled with sweetness. Our mornings commence with Bo showering Kelsey with affectionate kisses. Throughout the day, we exchange heartfelt text messages expressing our love. Each night, we embrace each other in a comforting hug before drifting off to sleep. In our leisure time, we enjoy strolling through the city, listening to music, and discovering new dining experiences. Kelsey finds joy in hand-crafting, drama-watching, and posing for photos taken by Bo. Meanwhile, Bo's interests include reading, soccer-watching, and engaging Kelsey in a myriad of intriguing conversations.
We've savored the taste of wine amid the autumnal charm of Napa, reclined on the winter beaches of Hawaii, admired blooming flowers in the springtime of Half Moon Bay, and engaged in summer kayaking adventures in Tahoe. From witnessing the midnight sun in Alaska at 11 PM to the shared challenge of spending two hours extricating our car from the winter snow in Tahoe, we've experienced a diverse range of moments. Anticipating the journey ahead, we eagerly look forward to creating more cherished scenes together in our shared life.
